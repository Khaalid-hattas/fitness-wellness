About FitWell Coach

FitWell Coach began as a collaboration between students at Life Choices Academy-YouthCode, united by a shared goal: to apply our HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills to a real-world project.

During development, we discovered a common passion that extended beyond code - fitness and wellness. Most of us integrate workouts and healthy habits into our daily lives, and that shared commitment became the foundation of this platform.

What started as a technical assignment evolved into FitWell Coach: a space where tchnology meets wellbeing, designed by students, for everyone pursuing a healthier lifestyle.

Our Mission

To bridge technology and wellness by creating accessible, student-built digital tools that empower individuals to take control of their fitness journey. We aim to prove healthy living and clean code can be built side by side.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform where young South African developers inspire healthier communities through technology. We envision FitWell coach growing from a student project into a trusted resource for anyone balancing life, studies, and wellness.
